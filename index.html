Hi! I'm Ezra Mariñas

I'm from BSIT-3R10. My team and I are developing a pet adoption app designed to streamline the adoption process for both rescue centers and adopters. The app helps rescue centers efficiently manage their animals by creating detailed profiles that highlight each pet’s temperament, needs, and care requirements. For adopters, it matches them with pets that fit their lifestyle and preferences, improving the chances of successful adoptions. By addressing issues like pets being overlooked or adopters facing mismatches, we aim to make the adoption process easier, more efficient, and rewarding for everyone involved.
